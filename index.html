§ 005 — FAQ

常见问题FAQ

数据安全吗?Is my data safe?

所有数据本地处理,不上传任何第三方云端。项目完成后按客户要求删除原始数据。All data is processed locally. No third-party cloud uploads. Raw data deleted upon project completion per your request.

多久能交付?How fast is delivery?

简单分析(描述性统计/相关)2-3 天,标准实证 5-7 天,复杂项目视具体情况沟通。Basic analysis: 2-3 days. Standard empirical: 5-7 days. Complex projects: discussed case by case.

能保证结果显著吗?Can you guarantee significant results?

不能,这也是我们的底线。数据说什么就是什么,我们不会为了"好看"而编造或操纵结果。No, and this is a firm principle. We report what the data shows. We never fabricate or manipulate results to achieve significance.

不满意怎么办?What if I'm not satisfied?

标准服务含 15 天免费售后,期内可沟通调整。我们的目标是交付到你满意为止。Standard service includes 15 days of free post-delivery support for adjustments. Our goal is your complete satisfaction.

怎么付款?How do I pay?

一次性付清,完成初稿后先交由平台代管,待客户收货好评后再解付给专业老师。Full payment upfront, held in platform escrow. Released to our analysts only after client confirms delivery and leaves positive feedback.

你们用什么工具?What tools do you use?

Python、R、MATLAB、SPSS、Stata 全套数据分析工具,根据项目需求灵活选用。所有代码随报告交付。Python, R, MATLAB, SPSS, Stata — full spectrum of data analysis tools, chosen per project needs. All code delivered with the report.
